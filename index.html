Recent Projects

Here are a few projects I have worked on!

Planetz

A Gamification of the project "diggr". The goal of the project is to open up research into Japanese game culture through database technology. Since the database was faulty, it was our task to make checking the database playful.

As project manager, I was responsible for the planning and implementation of the project. In addition to managing, I also took over a large part of the programming.

Role: Project Manager, Programmer

Visit diggr

Purple Sphere

Purple Sphere is a local-coop puzzle game, where one player plays as an human and the other player as a sphere. Both characters have different abilities, so they must work together to complete the puzzles.

Purple Sphere is a game I'm working on in my free time.

Bus Defense

In Bus Defense ghosts appear everywhere and try to take control of the bus. By moving your smartphone you can look around and find them. To defeat them you have to draw a line over the screen to hit them. Once you release your finger the hit is being executed.

This Project was part of Daimler's In-Car-Gaming-Challenge in which our team participated. Task was to present a pitch for a game that can be played in a car or bus. In addition to the pitch, we also created a small prototype.

Role: Programmer

Buddy's messy way Home

Meet Buddy, a discarded toy robot, who, on his way home, comes along a bunch of friends and adventures, forging bonds and memories along the way and finally finding his way home. Or so it should be. Nether the Player nor the poor souls inside are blessed with this turmoil of a game. But it's the hard times where bonds consolidate and home is more important than ever before.

This was my first participation in a Game Jam. We did not finish the game, but it was a lot of fun and I learned a lot.

Role: Programmer
